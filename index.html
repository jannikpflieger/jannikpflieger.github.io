About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I am a passionate quantum computing researcher and software developer currently pursuing my MSc in Computer Science at Technische Universität München (TUM), with a focus on HPC and quantum computing. I'm working on my Master Thesis on Lattice Surgery for Magic State Cultivation and have extensive experience implementing quantum algorithms and QEC codes.

I'm also pursuing an MSc in Politics & Technology at TUM, exploring the intersection of technology and society. As Department Lead QuantenTräume at PushQuantum e.V, I organize science communication events and public speaking presentations on quantum technology. My interests span quantum computing, AI, ML, traveling, mathematics, natural sciences, puzzle solving, and public speaking.
